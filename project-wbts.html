From 2009 to 2015, I worked on a number of eLearning and mLearning courses. Many of these self-paced courses focused on introducing new products to 40K+ retail employees via multiple platforms.

My audience was sales consultants, support reps, and line managers from internal and external retail channels. Courses were designed and developed in partnership with stakeholders from the field, marketing, sales operations, and OEMs.
